Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun. That means it's hot ---- four times hotter than boiling water !!!

Mariner 10

Mariner 10 was the first space probe to fly to Mercury. It left Earth in 1973 and flew past Mercury three times.

From Earth

Mercury is difficult to see from Earth because it is so close to the Sun and gets lost in its light. It is visible for a few hours after sunset and before sunrise.

Rocky Planets

Mercury is one of the rocky planets. It is covered in huge dents, called craters. Craters are made by big rocks, called asteroids, that crash into it.
