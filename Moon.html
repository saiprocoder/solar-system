Moon

Our Moon is our closest neighbour in space. It revolves around Earth and is the brightest object in the sky at night.

Eclipse

Sometimes the Moon moves right between the Sun and the Earth. It completely blocks out the light from the Sun for up to eight minutes. This is called an eclipse.

Moon Phases

It takes about 29.5 days for a full Moon to become full again. The changing shapes are called phases.
