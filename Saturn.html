Saturn

Saturn is a huge ball of gas that has no hard surface. It is famous for the amazing rings that surround it.

Giant eyeball

One of Saturn's moons, Mimas, looks just like a big eyeball because of the huge crater on it. This moon is small and could fit inside Texas.

Saturn's rings

The rings of Saturn are made up of billions of pieces of ice. Some of the size of a tiny speck of dust and some are as big as a car.
