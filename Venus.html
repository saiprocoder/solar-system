Venus

Venus is known as Earth's twin as it is a similar size and is made of similar rock.

Cloudy Days

Venus has a rocky landscape and yellow clouds. The clouds are so thick that sunlight never reaches the surface. Every day is cloudy on Venus.

Burning Hot

Venus is farther from the Sun than Mercury, yet it is hotter at 900°F (480°C). Venus rotates slowly, so the Sun heats it for months at a time without cooling nights. The clouds keep the heat in, so it stays very hot.
