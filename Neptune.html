Neptune

Neptune is a giant ball of liquid and gas that lies far away in our solar system.

Mystery Planet

Neptune was discovered when someone figured out that Uranus was being gravitionally pulled by another object, which turned out to be Neptune. It was first seen in 1846.

Super Storms

Neptune has the most violent weather in the solar system. Storms the size of Earth swirl around at huge speeds ----- ten times faster than our worst hurricanes.

Coldest place

Triton is Neptune's biggest moon. It is the coldest place in our solar system. It is so cold that the air has frozen around it.
