Jupiter

Jupiter is the biggest planet in our Solar System. It is so big that all the other planets could fit inside it, with room to spare.

The Gas Giant

Jupiter is the first of the four gas planets. Bright yellow and red clouds swirl around it. Jupiter has no hard surface at all. In fact, if you flew to Jupiter, you would find nowhere to land.

Jupiter's Moon

Jupiter has 63 rocky moons that orbit it. The four biggest moons are the size of small planets.

The Great Red Spot

The Great Red Spot is a giant, swirling storm that can be seen on surface of Jupiter. As the storm moves around, it swallows up other storms.
